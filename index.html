About Us

The Harrisburg Golf Course is located in Harrisburg, North Carolina,
about 10 miles northeast of uptown Charlotte. It is home to one of the
many prestigous golf clubs in the Charlotte area, including TPC Piper Glen,
Quail Hollow Country Club, and Carmel Country Club.

Ideal for all ages, Harrisburg Golf Course allows golfers to play from a
variety of tees, but also does provide a challenge for those
who are up to it by playing the back tees. Fast greens, along with the hilly fairways
and numerous bunkers make for a challenging, but fun experience out on the course.

Please use this website to book tee times, look at course rates,
or contact us using the navigation bar at the top of the page. Thanks!
